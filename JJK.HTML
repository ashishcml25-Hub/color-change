Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen (JJK) is a popular anime and manga series.

Main Characters

  • Yuji Itadori
  • Megumi Fushiguro
  • Nobara Kugisaki
  • Satoru Gojo

About

The story follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who joins a secret organization of Jujutsu Sorcerers to defeat powerful curses.
